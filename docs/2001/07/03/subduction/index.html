Subduction
Posted on Jul 3, 2001

“How do you turn around troubled lives when so many of our social problems involve human behavoir?” This quote from a Salon.com article. the article goes on to say the problems specific to human behavoir are “addiction and violence.” The question Salon.com raises, is what is the governments role? Well folks, I asked you a question… What is the governments role in policing human behavoir in terms of violence and addiction? What do we need to do to subdue the masses?
