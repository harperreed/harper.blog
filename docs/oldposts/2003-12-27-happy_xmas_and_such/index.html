happy xmas and such
Posted on Dec 27, 2003

So we had a great xmas. I am glad to be back but I was really happy to be able to spend christmas with my family. my cat had a hole in it. it was gross. but apparently it is getting filled in or has gotten filled in. it was really nasty. we all hung out and dressed up. my father wore his bling and I wore my fur - dylan was dressed normal. I learned a bunch of magic. it was awesome.

i really think that pinworms and botflys are gross. much grosser than holes in cats.

my thoughts go out to the knutson family.
