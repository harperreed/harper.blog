Jugglers of Death
Posted on Jul 4, 2002

USA USA USA USA USA USA USA USA USA USA

I sit in my kitchen once again.. having just got back from a juggling show at the Prodigal Son. It was a great time. It was incredible. The audience was receptive.. the juggling went well. We didn’t drop at any pivitol points.. heh. and we got contacts for more gigs.

I am very interested in what is going to happen in the marrow. I wonder if the “terrorists” will attack. I hope not, however it would be interesting to see what plans are foiled. Hopefully we don’t get punished for our sins. heh. wierd that we are at war with terror.
