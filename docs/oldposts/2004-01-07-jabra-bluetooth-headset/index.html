Jabra bluetooth headset
Posted on Jan 7, 2004

so the other day I decided to pick up a Jabra Freespeak bluetooth headset for my t610. When wearing it I feel as though I am in the future. I thought about getting the digital bluespoon but i felt bad paying more for the headset than i did for the phone. so i guess that will have to wait for when i get my 6600.

here is an awesome review of the bluespoon here is a poor review of the jabra

One of the factors i used in making my decision was reading Russell Beatties’s write up and it seemed like he was very happy with it. so i thought.. maybe it isn’t that bad.. and it is cheap.

Now if I could get it to pair appropriately with my desktop - I could maybe use it as a VOIP headset.. and call my friends all over the world!
