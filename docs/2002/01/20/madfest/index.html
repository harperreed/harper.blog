MadFest
Posted on Jan 20, 2002

So this weekend I went to an awesome juggling convention. It was called Madfest(Madison Festival). The jugglers there are some of the best in the entire world. They are all very nice and are quite talented.The show was incredible, the were at least 5 professional juggling acts and many more really great amateur acts. The MC was a lot better than QUad Cities and the humor was great. It was probably the best juggling show I have ever seen.

well I hope everyone has a good Marin Luther King, jr. day.
