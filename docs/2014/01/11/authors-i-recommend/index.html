Authors I recommend
Posted on Jan 11, 2014

I read a lot. I am also vocal about what I am reading. This morning I was asked to recommend a few authors/books. I decided to write down the authors I recommend the most.

In no order (other than alphabetical by first name. lol):

  • Alastair Reynolds
  • Charles Stross
  • China meiville
  • Dan Simmons
  • Daniel Suarez
  • Elizabeth Moon
  • Haruki Murakami
  • John Scalzi
  • Iain Banks
  • Lauren Beukes
  • Paolo Bacigalupi
  • Philip K. Dick
  • Ramez Naam
  • Robin Hobb
  • Steven Brust
  • Vernor Vinge
  • William Hertling

Read these authors. Enjoy them. Have fun.

I also have a constantly updated list of all of the authors I have read.
