stupid sleep patterns..
Posted on Jan 5, 2004

I ended up starting a second book.. but I put it away with fear that if I didn’t I woudl finish it too and it would be 8 in the morning. well I supposed I should goto bed. I don’t really feel like it. but I can feel my energy level dropping as my body realizes it has food. ahhh.. food…
