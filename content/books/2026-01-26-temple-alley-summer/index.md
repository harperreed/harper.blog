Published as an illustrated edition, "Temple Alley Summer" by Sachiko Kashiwaba is a celebrated work that has captured the hearts of young readers and critics alike. It boasts several accolades, including the 2022 Mildred L. Batchelder Award and the prestigious SCBWI Crystal Kite Award. This enchanting novel invites readers into a thrilling narrative filled with elements of fantasy and suspense, weaving together a story about the living dead, magical artifacts, and the bonds of friendship.

The story follows Kazu, a seemingly ordinary boy who stumbles into extraordinary circumstances one summer. His encounters begin with a mysterious girl in a white dress, Akari, who appears in his life unexpectedly. As Kazu navigates the peculiarities of their interactions, he learns that Akari's existence may not conform to the ordinary laws of life and death. With his summer project centered around Kimyo Temple—an ancient site linked to a legend of resurrecting the dead—Kazu embarks on a quest alongside Akari to uncover the truth behind her enigmatic appearance.

As they delve deeper into the temple’s secrets, they face mysterious adults and must decipher an unfinished story from Akari's past that could hold the key to her fate. With engaging illustrations by Miho Satake enriching the reading experience, this tale combines whimsical storytelling with a sense of urgency as Kazu must protect Akari before time runs out.
