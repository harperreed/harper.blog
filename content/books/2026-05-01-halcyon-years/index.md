In 'Halcyon Years', acclaimed author Alastair Reynolds, known for his masterful storytelling in the Revelation Space universe, delivers a gripping murder mystery set in the outer reaches of space. The narrative centers on Yuri Gagarin, a private investigator who makes ends meet by taking on small-time cases. When he boards the starship Halcyon, which carries thousands of passengers across the cosmos, his mundane life takes a turbulent turn.

Hired by the enigmatic Ruby Red, Yuri is thrust into an investigation surrounding a high-profile death linked to an elite family aboard the starship. But just as he starts to dig deeper, he faces a warning from another mysterious woman, Ruby Blue. As he navigates a web of intrigue and secrets, the stakes rise, forcing Yuri into a deadly game where every move could be his last.

This novel is a testament to Reynolds’s ability to blend science fiction with classic noir elements, keeping readers on the edge of their seats with twists and turns that challenge their perceptions of trust and loyalty.
